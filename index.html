WELCOME

Welcome!
It's a great privilege to have you visiting and hoped to see you becoming a member of this wonderful family.
LILY OF THE VALLEY is a place of worship to find people passionate about God's kingdom. We are committed to life-transforming prayers, soul-lifting worship, and the in-depth teaching of the word of God. At Lily of the Valley, our goal is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ, win souls, and raise disciples for God's kingdom. Our primary mission is to make heaven.
We welcome you to be a part of this assembly of children of God in Baasala, Asero. Lily of the Valley is a community of Christians from various backgrounds, tribes, professions, and cultures striving to be Christ-like. We are a family passionate about impacting our community via our spiritual activities. Our strength is based on the joy of serving God, unity of purpose, and being a positive influence to our community.
If you desire to be an instrument in the hands of God, Lily of the Valley is the place to be. If you thirst for the knowledge of God, your home is Lily of the Valley. If you want a church where your children will grow in the wisdom and the knowledge of God, do not look any further.
Join this great family as we together make the difference the world is looking for.
Come and taste of the goodness of God.
Remain blessed in Jesus name.

VISION AND MISSION

As received by the General Overseer (G.O.), Pastor E. A. Adeboye, and communicated to the Headquarters leaders, our vision and mission statement shall remain intact, with a qualifying addendum in view of the peculiarity of the demography in the African Region. They are as follows:

  • To make heaven.
  • To take as many people with us.
  • To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.


To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.


We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.

ABOUT US

The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), Lily of the Valley is a parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) - a global Church with Pastor Enoch Adejare Adeboye as its General Overseer..

The Lord has continually blessed His church with wonderful people with great minds. We are a young, growing, multi-dynamic and vibrant church, and we believe:

  • You are very special.
    "But you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, God’s special possession, that you may declare the praises of him who called you out of darkness into his wonderful light" (I Peter 2:9)
  • You are His house.
    "Do you not know that your bodies are temples of the Holy Spirit, who is in you, whom you have received from God? You are not your own" (1 Corinthians 6:19)
  • You have been elected as the light of the world by His grace.
    "You are the light of the world. A city that is set on a hill cannot be hid" (Matthew 5: 14)
  • You are born to shine as light out of this dark world.
    "In him was life; and the life was the light of men. And the light shineth in darkness; and the darkness comprehended it not" (John 1:4-5)
  • You are created to mould destinies and to show forth His glory.
    "And God said, Let us make man in our image, after our likeness:" (Gen.1:28a)
  • You are very important to us and to the world as a whole.
    "For the earnest expectation of the creature waiteth for the manifestation of the sons of God" (Romans 8:19)

RCCG Lily of the Valley is a vibrant church filled with young adults and singles. Are you desiring to associate with our congregation, there are different arms of ministry to help you become grounded in the Lord and be a part of the family of God. Below are ministries within the church.

Women's Ministry

This is the women's arm of the church, a forum for both the married and unmarried women. The group is actively engaged in helping women feel uplifted in the service of the Lord and becoming virtuous women in their homes. Some of the group's activities include:

  • Prayer meeting twice a month
  • Yearly women conference
  • Outdoor activities

The aims of the ministry are as follows:

  • To raise complete woman spiritually, physically, emotionally and socially glorifying God
  • To express godly love to our family, church and community
  • To influence our community positively for Jesus
  • To positively engage women through expertise building exercises, wellbeing and business advancement talks
  • To love and serve our God in this era

Children/Teens Ministry

This ministry encompasses the future generation of Church leaders. It is a team of Children and Teens under eighteen years of age.
"Train up your child in the way he should go; and when he is old, he will not depart from it" (Proverb 22:6).

In our kids and Teens' church, we intend to prepare up our up and coming era of genuine best achievers in every aspect of life. We give a safe and fun learning condition for every one of our children in the kids and Teens' church. Our child's program is a road where kids take in more about scriptural ethics, comprehend the way that there is a God, who made them, thinks about them, adores them, can address every one of their issues and furthermore figuring out how to do the correct things to remain out of inconvenience in these last days!

Our weekly activities include:

  • Bible lesson and Memory verse: Each week we work through RCCG, junior church manual for our weekly lessons and memory verses. Children are encouraged to come in with their bibles and writing journals, learn at least one bible verse weekly. Becoming more Christ centred is our primary target for all kids attending our children's church.
  • Prayer time: Every child has an opportunity to lead the group in prayers as appropriate for the child's age.
  • Play time

Every child is encouraged to participate in all our children's ministrations such as Christmas carols, drama, choreography etc.

Prayer Ministry

At RCCG Lily of the Valley, we believe in prayer!
"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus" (Philippians 4:6 -7).

What are your challenges? As a church, we believe that prayer is the answer. You can make your prayer request online and you can likewise drop your prayer request In the prayer boxing at the church. For emergency prayers, you can call our prayer line or any of the lines on our contact page.
